First, GAD exams usually don’t just test memorization—they check if you understand **concepts, laws, and real-life applications** about gender equality and development.

Here are the **most common topics and how they usually appear in tests**:

---

## 1. Basic Concepts (very common in multiple choice)

You should know these clearly:

* **Sex vs Gender**

  * Sex = biological (male/female)
  * Gender = socially constructed roles (masculine/feminine)

* **Gender Equality**

  * Equal rights, responsibilities, and opportunities for all genders

* **Gender Equity**

  * Fair treatment based on needs (not always identical treatment)

* **Gender Stereotypes**

  * Fixed ideas about what men/women “should” do

?? Example question:

> Which best describes gender equity?
> A. Treating everyone exactly the same
> B. Giving extra support based on needs
> C. Biological differences between male and female
> D. Traditional roles of men and women
> **Answer: B**

---

## 2. GAD Laws and Policies (important in the Philippines)

If you're in Philippine classes, you often need to know:

* **RA 9710 – Magna Carta of Women**
* **RA 11313 – Safe Spaces Act (Bawal Bastos Law)**
* **RA 9262 – Anti-Violence Against Women and Their Children Act**
* **RA 7877 – Anti-Sexual Harassment Act**

?? Example question:

> Which law protects women from all forms of discrimination?
> **Answer: RA 9710**

---

## 3. Gender Issues in Society

Common topics:

* Gender-based violence
* Workplace discrimination
* LGBTQ+ inclusion
* Unequal pay
* Early marriage / teenage pregnancy

?? Example question:

> What is an example of gender discrimination?
> A. Equal pay for equal work
> B. Hiring only men for leadership roles
> C. Free education for all
> D. Health programs for everyone
> **Answer: B**

---

## 4. Gender Mainstreaming (important essay topic)

This means:

> Integrating gender perspective into all programs, policies, and projects.

Used in:

* Schools
* Government planning
* Community programs

---

## 5. Common Essay Questions

You might be asked to explain:

* “Why is gender equality important in development?”
* “How can schools promote Gender and Development?”
* “Give examples of gender stereotypes and how to reduce them.”

---

If you’re preparing for a test, focus on:

* Definitions (sex, gender, equity vs equality)
* Laws (especially RA 9710 and RA 11313)
* Real-life examples of gender issues
* Application questions (not just memorization)
