True or False: People who are asymptomatic cannot infect others.
Fast answer

False.

People who are asymptomatic (showing no symptoms) can still infect others. This has been well documented with diseases like COVID-19, where individuals can spread the virus without ever feeling sick.

Question 23 Select one:
