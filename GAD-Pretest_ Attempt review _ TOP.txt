Which situation shows gender bias in the workplace?


Question 1 Answer
a.
Despite having similar skills and experience, male candidates were preferred over female applicants.
b.
In a financial institution, both men and women get equal pay and benefits for comparable job positions.
c.
There is an equal opportunity for promotion to both men and women in the workplace.
d.
Joining the army is for anyone who wants to protect their country regardless of gender.
Preventing ones partner from having an occupation or a job is considered as _____________.

The correct answer is:

**a. Despite having similar skills and experience, male candidates were preferred over female applicants.**

This situation clearly shows gender bias because one gender is being favored over another even when qualifications are equal. The other options describe fair or equal treatment, not bias.

Question 3 Answer
a.

b.

c.

d.
During lockdowns due to the pandemic, the government provides "ayuda" to every family.

The correct answer is:

**d. During lockdowns due to the pandemic, the government provides "ayuda" to every family.**

This is an example of **equality** (everyone receives the same support), not **equity**. Equity involves giving additional help based on specific needs, which is shown in the other options.
