The following situations are examples of equity, EXCEPT
Question 4 Answer
a.
The teacher arrange extra classes for the academically weak students
b.
Workers who receives lower salaries get tax exemptions.
c.
Healthcare workers provides extra care and nutrition to the malnourished children.
d.
During lockdowns due to the pandemic, the government provides "ayuda" to every family.

The correct answer is:

**d. During lockdowns due to the pandemic, the government provides "ayuda" to every family.**

This is because **equity** means giving more support to those who need it most. Options **a, b, and c** all provide additional help to disadvantaged groups, while option **d** describes equal distribution (everyone receives the same), not equity.
